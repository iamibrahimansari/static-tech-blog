Write a program in C programming language to find prime factors of given number?

Prime factorization is a way of expressing a number as a product of its prime factors. A prime number is a number that has exactly two factors, 1 and the number itself. For example, if we take the number 30. We know that 30 = 5 × 6, but 6 is not a prime number. The number 6 can further be factorized as 2 × 3, where 2 and 3 are prime numbers. Therefore, the prime factorization of 30 = 2 × 3 × 5, where all the factors are prime numbers.

You are given a natural number greater than or equal to 2 because negative numbers and 0 as well as 1 are not able to express as product of prime numbers. Your job is to find all the factors of given this natural number if given natural number is greater than or equal to 2 otherwise print "Your input is invalid".

Example 1

Input

Enter a natural number(>=2): 10

Output

Prime factors of 10 are: 2, 5.

Explanation

Number 10 can be express as product of 2 and 5 because 2 and 5 are prime factors of 10.

Example 2

Input

Enter a natural number(>=2): 30

Output

Prime factors of 30 are: 2, 3, 5.

Explanation

Number 30 can be express as product of 2, 3 and 5 because 2, 3 and 5 are prime factors of 30.

Example 3

Input

Enter a natural number(>=2): 1

Output

Your input is invalid.

Explanation

Number 1 can't be express as product prime factors because smallest prime factor is 2 which is greater than 1.
